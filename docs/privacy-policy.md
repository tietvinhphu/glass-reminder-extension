# Privacy Policy — Glass Reminder Extension

**Last updated:** June 12, 2026

---

## Overview

Glass Reminder is a browser extension that helps you set and manage personal reminders directly in Microsoft Edge. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

**In short: Glass Reminder does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.**

---

## Data We Collect

Glass Reminder collects **no personal data**.

All reminders you create (title, date, time, notes) are stored **exclusively on your device** using the browser's built-in local storage (`chrome.storage.local`). This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to any server.

---

## Data Storage

| Data | Where stored | Sent to server? |
|---|---|---|
| Reminders (title, time, notes) | Your device only | Never |
| Notification preferences | Your device only | Never |

---

## Network Access

Glass Reminder does **not** make any network requests. It does not connect to the internet, external APIs, or any third-party services.

---

## Third-Party Services

Glass Reminder does not use:
- Analytics or tracking (no Google Analytics, no Mixpanel, etc.)
- Advertising networks
- Crash reporting services
- Any external APIs

---

## Permissions Used

| Permission | Why it's needed |
|---|---|
| `storage` | Save your reminders on your device |
| `alarms` | Trigger notifications at the right time |
| `notifications` | Show reminder alerts |

---

## Data Deletion

To delete all your data, simply remove the extension from your browser. All locally stored reminders are deleted automatically.

You can also clear individual reminders directly within the extension.

---

## Children's Privacy

Glass Reminder does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

---

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
